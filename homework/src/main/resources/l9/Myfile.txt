5 reasons why reading is efficient way to improve English or any other language.
There they are;
It helps you to improve vocabulary and grammar at the same time.
It helps you to understand how the language works.
It helps your brain to get used to the language and don’t perceive it as a foreign.
It helps you to think in English.
It helps you to learn cultural expressions (any country, any century)
Bonus;
Reading is a good way for stress reduction.
Reading improves your memory.
Reading improves concentration.
